What am I searching?

Library Search is BGSU’s University Libraries search tool. By default, it returns results for materials the library owns or subscribes to, including books, articles, and more. You can refine your search by using the drop-down menu in the search box and selecting from the different search categories.

  • BGSU Catalog – Includes physical books, journals, and materials at BGSU. Can be further limited to the Browne Popular Culture Library (BPCL), Center for Archival Collections (CAC), Curriculum Resource Center (CRC) or Music Library and Bill Schurk Sound Archives (MLBSSA).
  • Browne Popular Culture Library (BPCL) – Limits the search to materials in the BPCL.
  • Center for Archival Collections (CAC) – Limits the search to materials in the CAC.
  • Curriculum Resource Center (CRC) – Limits the search to materials in the CRC.
  • Music Library and Bill Schurk Sound Archives (MLBSSA) – Limits search to materials in the MLBSSA.
  • BGSU, OhioLINK and More – Expands the search to include materials available to request from other OhioLINK libraries and beyond. Items can be requested through the interlibrary loan (ILL) process and delivered to you digitally or physically, at no cost.
  • e-Books – Limits results to e-books the library owns or subscribes to.

Why log in?
  • Renew books and materials.
  • Create favorites lists.
  • Export citations to RefWorks or other citation managers.
  • View full search results (some databases only show results when you’re signed in).
